I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva to visually represent important concepts related 
to digital awareness for students. The infographic is titled “Digital Literacy for Students” and highlights
key areas such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional
online presence, and email etiquette.

The design uses a structured layout with different colored sections to make the information easy to read and visually 
appealing. Each section includes simple points and icons to help viewers quickly understand the concepts. For example, 
the infographic explains how tools like Google Docs, GitHub, and LinkedIn are useful for students, and it also emphasizes
safety practices like using strong passwords and avoiding unknown links.

While creating the infographic, I found it interesting to organize information in a concise and creative way. The main challenge 
was maintaining a balance between design and content without making it cluttered. Overall, this task helped me understand how to 
present information effectively using digital tools.
